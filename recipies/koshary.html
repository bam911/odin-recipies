koshary

Description:

Koshary is Egypt’s beloved national dish and ultimate comfort food. It is a hearty, budget-friendly vegetarian meal that combines layers of rice, lentils, pasta, and chickpeas, topped with a spiced tomato sauce and a tangy vinegar-garlic dressing. Crispy fried onions crown the dish, giving it a satisfying crunch. Koshary is famous for its mix of textures and bold flavors, and it’s served everywhere in Egypt—from street stalls to restaurants.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Brown or green lentils
  • Macaroni or small pasta
  • Chickpeas (boiled or canned)
  • Tomato sauce (with garlic, cumin, vinegar, chili optional)
  • Garlic-vinegar dressing (da’a)
  • Fried onions (crispy)
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook Lentils and Rice
  2. Cook Pasta
  3. Prepare Chickpeas
  4. Make Tomato Sauce
  5. Make Garlic-Vinegar Dressing (Da’a)
  6. Prepare Fried Onions
  7. Assemble the Dish
