Pizza

Description:

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough baked with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as vegetables, meats, and herbs. It is one of the most popular foods worldwide and is enjoyed in countless variations, from classic Margherita to loaded meat loversâ€™ styles.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Fresh basil or oregano
  • Toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.
  • Salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Preheat the Oven
  3. Spread Sauce on Dough
  4. Add Cheese and Toppings
  5. Bake the Pizza
  6. Slice and Serve
