shawarma

Description:

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food made from thinly sliced marinated meat (usually beef, lamb, or chicken) that is stacked on a vertical rotisserie and slow-cooked. As it roasts, the outer layers are shaved off and typically served in warm pita bread or flatbread, often with garlic sauce, tahini, or hummus. Fresh vegetables, pickles, and fries are commonly added inside, making it flavorful, juicy, and filling.

Ingredients

    • Meat (chicken, beef, or lamb)
    • Yogurt
    • Olive oil
    • Lemon juice
    • Vinegar
    • Garlic
    • Spices: cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom, black pepper, salt
    • Flatbread or pita
    • Sauces: garlic sauce (toum), tahini, or hummus
    • Fresh fillings: lettuce, tomatoes, onions, cucumbers, parsley
    • Pickles
    • Fries (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade
  2. Marinate the Meat
  3. Cook the Meat
  4. Prepare Sauces & Fillings
  5. Assemble Shawarma
